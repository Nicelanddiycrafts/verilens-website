Values

Why we built VeriLens

Product visuals shape buying choices and AI generated media makes it hard to know what is real. VeriLens gives shoppers clear, quick signals that users can understand without needing any technical knowledge.

Model

What happens after someone taps Scan listing.

1

Capture

Find the key listing photos or clips.

2

Check visual consistency

Run light visual checks to spot unusual patterns often linked to heavy editing or synthetic media.

3

Share indicators

Give a risk label with short reasons in plain words.

4

Highlight

Show a color coded heatmap pointing to the areas that look most unusual.

How we describe the model

VeriLens uses a set of lightweight explainable visual checks to flag unusual patterns often linked to heavy editing or synthetic media. We share indicators with brief reasons and a heatmap so shoppers can make informed choices without needing to know technical terms.

Fast

Built for real time use while shopping.

Explainable

Short reasons and a visual heatmap make results easy to read.

Guidance

Safeguards reduce false alarms by looking for natural photo cues.

Feasibility and impact

Built as a working MVP and refined through real shopping runs.

Feasibility

  • Working MVP browser extension that runs on live listings
  • Light CPU friendly checks keep scans quick
  • Explainable results with a score reasons and a heatmap

Impact

  • Reduce misleading purchases and shopping frustration
  • Improve consumer confidence at decision time
  • Promote transparency as AI generated product media grows

How we measure success

  • Test across many listings and adjust to reduce false alarms
  • Listen to shopper feedback to improve clarity
  • Watch adoption and returning users to be sure it stays useful

Roadmap

  • Expand testing across more categories and platforms
  • Improve clarity of reasons and heatmap visuals
  • Explore advanced models for edge cases while keeping the simple flow
